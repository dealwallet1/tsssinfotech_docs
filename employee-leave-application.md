# Leave Management leave application(End-to-End Guide)

## Overview

This guide explains the complete process for an employee to apply for leave in Odoo, from login to successful submission.

---

## 1. Login to Odoo

- Open your Odoo application  
- Enter your **Username** and **Password**  
- Click **Login**

---

## 2. Open Time Off Module

- After login, go to the main dashboard  
- Click on **Time Off**

---

## 3. Create a New Leave Request

- Click on **My Time Off**  
- Click the **New** button  

---

## 4. Fill Leave Details

### Select Time Off Type

Choose the appropriate leave type:

- Casual Leave  
- Sick Leave  
- Work From Home  
- Comp Off  

---

### Select Leave Dates

- Choose the **Start Date**  
- Choose the **End Date**  
- Click **Apply**

---

### Enter Reason

Provide a clear reason for leave:

- Medical Appointment  
- Family Function  
- Personal Work  

---

### Add Attachment (Optional)

- Upload supporting documents if required  
- Example: Medical certificate  

---

## 5. Save Leave Request

- Click **Save**  
- Ensure all details are correct  

---

## 6. Submit Leave Request

- Click **Submit**

---

## 7. Submission Confirmation

After submission:

- Leave status will be: **To Approve**  
- Request is successfully sent to manager/HR  

---

## Final Status

✅ Leave application successfully submitted.  

The request is now waiting for approval.
