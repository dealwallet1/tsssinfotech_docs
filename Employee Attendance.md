# Employee Attendance-End-to-End Guide


--------------------------------------------------------------------

## Overview

This guide explains how employees can mark attendance using Kiosk Mode with barcode scanning in Odoo.

---

## 1. Login to Odoo

- Open your Odoo application
- Enter your **Username** and **Password**
- Click **Login**

---

## 2. Open Attendance Module

- After login, go to the main dashboard
- Click on **Attendances**

---

## 3. Open Kiosk Mode

- Inside the Attendance module
- Click on **Kiosk Mode**

---

## 4. Select Barcode Scanning

- Choose **Barcode Scanning**

---

## 5. Check-In

- Go to the kiosk device
- Scan your barcode

- The system will:
  - Identify you
  - Record your check-in time

---

## 6. Check-Out

- Scan your barcode again when leaving

- The system will:
  - Record your check-out time

---

## Note

- No login is required in Kiosk Mode
- Always use your correct barcode

---

## Conclusion

Employees can quickly mark attendance using barcode scanning without logging into the system.
